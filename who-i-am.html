Who am I?

I really like to create things. All sorts of things. Let this be handcrafted projects, digital projects, movie making or just doodling. I like the idea of making something from scratch, where, on the way, I have to solve countless problems. I have a couple of short documentaries and film projects under my belt, where I was a director, editor, camera operator, and a very bad actor. :)

I studied at the University of Ljubljana in the fields of Cultural and Communication studies. This helped me to develop great empathy and understanding of other people. I try to put a lot of thought into how things will be used. I am currently focused on the basics of UX design, creating digital products in a lean and agile way.

I would describe myself as a very responsible, precise and easygoing person. I enjoy working in a team where people collaborate, but I can also be very independent when there is a need for that. Professionally, I see myself in a creative field, where I can contribute to the product success with my design and problem-solving skills.
