Film

Previous Next

My passion for filmmaking goes far back to the years when I was a teenager in primary school. Our technical teacher organized film workshops and taught us about animation. In that time I got to know with the camera and participated in making a few fiction films and two short animations. Later in high school and during my university years I volunteered on different film festivals and participated in many film workshops. That kind of workshops are a great way to learn how to work in a team you didn't know before, how to work under pressure, since the time is always limited, and how to produce something even without a complete equipment. And the most important, you get to know people with the same interests and have FUN! :)

Do-it-yourself

I love "do-it-yourself" projects. Why? It's the thrill what can come up from under your own hands, it's the infinite creativity of your mind, and it's FUN FUN FUN! :) And the best part is when you watch and use the product you made every day. Some of the things that came from under my own hands are the 3D Settlers of Catan, bathroom wooden furniture, a coffee table, and crazy Lego costumes.

CABARET DANCE I love to dance. It is how I feed my soul and release my everyday stress. I dance since my childhood, tried different styles, from cheerleading, hip-hop, and jazz to my latest obsession - cabaret dance. It combines everything I like - great music, acrobatic perfection, and the most important - telling a story. I am one of the members of Divas de Cabaret dance group.
